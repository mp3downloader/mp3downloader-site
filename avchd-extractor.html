AVCHD-Extractor

AVCHD-Extractor can extract avchd videos lesslessly. It can convert avchd files captured by cameras such as Sony, Cannon, Panasonic etc.
AVCHD-Extractor supports converting AVCHD to MP4, MOV, and M4V files for playback on your Mac, iPhone, iPad, or importing video editing software such as FCP, iMovie, Premiere and more.

Features
Input: AVCHD package file.
Output: MP4, MOV, M4V, MTS.
Easy to use: 1 Drag&Drop the avchd file, 2 Select an output format, 3 Click 'Start' button.
