Simple DVD Creator

Simple DVD Creator is a simple and easy to use video DVD maker software. It can convert and burn common videos to DVD discs. Then you can play on the DVD player, or share them with your friends.

Simple DVD Creator also supports the recording of SRT subtitle files.

Features
Supported input video format: mp4, m4v, mov, mpeg, mpg, 3gp, avi, asf, flv, f4v, ogg, ogv, mjpeg, mkv, mxf, rm, rmvb, ts, m2ts, mts, wmv
Output: Video-DVD disc, ISO image.
