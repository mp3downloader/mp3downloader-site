Auto-Screen-Brightness

Auto-Screen-Brightness is a menu bar application that automatically sets the screen brightness based on your screen contents.
Auto-Screen-Brightness will automatically brighten the screen when you're looking at a dark window, and it'll automatically darken the screen when you're looking at a bright window. This makes for a much more pleasant experience, especially at night.
